Key Observations

HighVolWR is the outlier. At Δ 0.10 (deep OTM calls around $0.27), it leverages a 69% equity win rate into +76.3% ROI. When the trade wins, cheap OTM options deliver 5-10x payoffs. When it loses, you only lose the small premium. No other rule can profitably use OTM options because their ~50% win rates can't overcome theta decay.

Short rules need ITM puts (Δ 0.70). At $3.27 avg entry with 39 contracts, ITM puts closely track the underlying's downward move. OTM puts fail for shorts because most fade trades capture small moves that don't push cheap puts into the money before expiry.

2024 is profitable under options — unlike equity. The equity strategy went flat (-$20) in 2024's low-vol bull grind, but the optimal options portfolio made +$102K. The ITM short puts at Δ 0.70 captured enough on fade trades while capping losses on the persistent trend days that killed equity shorts.

Risk is strictly bounded. Worst single trade: -$10,000 (the premium). Worst single day: -$32,614. Compare to equity where a single trade on $3M notional could lose $100K+ with no stop loss. Options naturally solve the "no stop loss" problem.

2025 was the breakout year. +$632K (21.4% ROI) driven by elevated volatility creating favorable option pricing and larger moves that amplified option payoffs. This is consistent with the strategy's design — momentum rules profit most in high-vol regimes, and options magnify those gains.
