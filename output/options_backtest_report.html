Key Observations

HighVolWR is the standout options candidate. Its 69% equity win rate translates to massive leverage at deep OTM (Δ 0.10): +38.3% ROI on premium. The high win rate means OTM options pay off frequently enough to overcome theta decay. No other rule comes close at low deltas.

Short rules strongly prefer ITM options (Δ 0.70). Both PriorDay Strong shorts are most profitable at Δ 0.70, where the option behaves more like the underlying but still caps downside. This makes sense — short trades need the option to track the move closely, and OTM puts bleed premium on the many small-loss days.

Long momentum rules work best at ATM (Δ 0.50). The PriorDay Weak rules and GapLarge all optimize at Δ 0.50, balancing leverage vs. theta cost. Going further OTM destroys these rules because their ~50% win rate can't overcome the premium decay on losing trades.

Risk is strictly capped. Every trade's maximum loss is the $10K premium paid — no gap risk, no overnight exposure, no stop-loss slippage. This is the core advantage over equity positions where a 3.3% adverse move on a $3M position = $100K loss.

2025 was the big winner. The 0.50 delta portfolio made +$577K in 2025 alone (through Feb 2026), driven by elevated vol creating favorable option pricing. 2021 and 2023 were tough years — consistent with equity underperformance in those periods plus additional theta drag.
